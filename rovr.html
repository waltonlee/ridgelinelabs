Current Project - RoVR

Your own friendly companion in VR

In our first VR project, we are constructing an environment where you can train, feed and play with an interactive pet much like one in real life. Your pet will be able to react to various interactions such as petting, fetch and speech. As your pet progresses you will be able to take it on walks and enter competitions to earn items with which to groom and dress up your pet.
