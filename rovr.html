Current Project - RoVR

Your own friendly canine companion in VR

RoVR is a dog ownership simulation game for the HTC Vive and Oculus Rift. Aimed at all audiences, RoVR explores the viability of forming emotionally significant relationships with virtual entities. We are constructing a world where players can raise, feed, compete, and befriend a VR dog similarly to a real one. Virtual dogs will be able to react to various interactions such as petting, fetch, voice commands, and many more. Aside from taking care of the dog, players will be able to go on walks in a beautiful environment, enter competitions to earn gold and items with which to groom and dress up with, and explore distant lands.

Many people have formed emotionally significant relationships with their dogs. We want to let people experience that connection using virtual reality.
